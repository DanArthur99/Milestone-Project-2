Rules of the Game

The aim of the game is to be the first to empty your hand!

The card you lay must be the same suit or the same number as the top card of the Pile

If you cannot lay a card, you must draw one from the pile.


Special Cards:

2: If a player lays down a 2, the next player must either draw 2 cards from the pile, or lay down a 2 themselves. This would accumulate the number the cards the next player has to lay down to 4, and so on.

Ace: An Ace changes the direction of play. If this is also an Ace of Spades, then the next player after the change of direction must pick up 6 cards, unless they have an Ace of Spades themselves

Jack: If a player lays down a Jack, the next player's turn is skipped.

8: An 8 is the only card that can be layed at anytime regardless of suit or number (unless the previous player has layed a 2, or an Ace of Spades). Upon laying an 8, the player can choose the suit that the next player must lay (this can however be overruled if the next player has an 8 themselves)
